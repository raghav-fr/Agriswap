About AgriSwap

AgriSwap is a digital platform designed to make farm machinery more accessible and affordable for small and medium farmers. Instead of each farmer investing huge capital into equipment that is used only for a few weeks, AgriSwap enables a sharing-based model where machinery can be rented on demand.

For Farmers

Affordable access

Rent machinery at a fraction of the purchase cost and reduce cost of cultivation without compromising on timeliness and quality of operations.

For Owners

Extra income

Generate additional revenue from tractors and other implements that might otherwise stay idle for most of the year.

For Ecosystem

Efficient utilization

Encourages better use of existing machinery, reducing unnecessary duplication, fuel wastage and financial stress in rural communities.

How AgriSwap Works

The platform is simple to use for both farmers and equipment owners. The process is designed to be transparent, safe and convenient.

Register

Farmers and equipment owners sign up with basic details such as village, type of machine and contact number.

List or search

Owners list their machinery with availability and approximate rent. Farmers search for nearby machines based on their requirement.

Book & confirm

Farmers place a booking request for selected dates. The owner confirms and both parties receive booking details.

Use & rate

After the work is completed, both farmer and owner provide ratings and feedback to build trust on the platform.

Key Features

AgriSwap focuses on solving real problems faced by farmers in accessing machinery, while giving owners a simple way to manage their services.

Local matchmaking

Connects farmers with equipment owners within their own village or nearby clusters to minimize travel time and cost.

Transparent pricing

Standardized rental rates and clear terms avoid confusion and disputes at the time of payment.

Flexible booking

Allows booking per hour, per day or per operation based on the agreement between farmer and owner.

Multi-channel access

Farmers can use a mobile app, WhatsApp interface or a local AgriSwap representative to place requests.

Ratings & reviews

Builds trust through feedback and ratings, helping farmers choose reliable machines and operators.

Support & guidance

Provides basic assistance on machinery selection and usage through help content or local partners.

Who is AgriSwap for?

  • Small and medium farmers who cannot afford to purchase tractors and other machines.
  • Tractor and implement owners looking to earn additional income by renting out their assets.
  • Custom Hiring Centres (CHCs) and agri entrepreneurs who already provide services but want a digital channel.
  • Farmer Producer Organisations (FPOs) managing shared resources for member farmers.

Why choose AgriSwap?

  • Reduces capital burden and risk for farmers by shifting from ownership to access.
  • Helps ensure timely operations, improving crop yields and productivity.
  • Improves utilization of existing machinery and supports rural income diversification.
  • Creates opportunities for rural youth to act as local service coordinators or agents.
